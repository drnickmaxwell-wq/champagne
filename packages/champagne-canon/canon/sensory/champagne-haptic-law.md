# Champagne Touch & Haptic Interaction Law

Haptics in Champagne are barely there – just enough to confirm intention and safety.

No strong buzzes, no long pulses, no gaming feedback.

---

## 1. Haptic Types

### 1. Micro-Tap

- Duration: 8–12ms
- Soft, barely perceptible
- Use: button taps, nav changes

### 2. Micro-Pressure

- Duration: 14–18ms
- Slightly deeper vibration
- Use: confirming selections, opening cards

### 3. Soft-Exhale Vibration

- Duration: 20–30ms
- Gentle fade-out
- Use: dismissals, back navigation

### 4. Ceremonial Pulse

- Duration: 40–50ms
- Two-phase pulse (“micro bloom”)
- Use: plan accepted, smile journey complete, major milestones

---

## 2. Behaviour Laws

1. Haptics are always optional and respect system/device settings.
2. Never combine more than one haptic pattern for a single interaction.
3. Ceremonial Pulse is rare and must align with a Ritual Motion + soft sound.
4. Emergency, consent, and high-focus screens may reduce or disable haptics if they increase stress.
