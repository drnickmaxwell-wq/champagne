# Champagne Dental 3D Material Spec

Champagne dental materials must look:
- clinically correct
- physically plausible
- emotionally luxurious
- never glossy or fake-plastic

Lighting integrates with HDR Lab canon.

---

## 1. Material Families

### 1.1 Enamel (Natural Tooth)
- Semi-translucent outer layer
- Warm internal scattering
- Subtle micro-texture (perikymata hint)
- Blue tint at incisal edge
Material ID: `material.dental.enamel.natural`

---

### 1.2 Lithium Disilicate (Emax-style Ceramic)
- Crisp glass-ceramic sheen
- Medium translucency
- Micro-shimmer edges
Material ID: `material.dental.ceramic.lithiumDisilicate`

---

### 1.3 Zirconia (Mono / Multilayer)
- Dense, low translucency
- Thin, cool highlight band
Material IDs:
- `material.dental.zirconia.mono`
- `material.dental.zirconia.multi`

---

### 1.4 Composite Resin
- Softer, warmer highlights
- Minimal translucency
- Micro filler visibility at macro zoom
Material ID: `material.dental.composite.universal`

---

### 1.5 Titanium (Implant Components)
- Brushed, cool steel
- High clarity reflections
- No chrome shine
Material ID: `material.dental.metal.titaniumGrade5`
