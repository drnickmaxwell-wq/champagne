# Champagne Smile Simulator OS

A cinematic, gesture-based smile visualisation engine built around physical enamel behaviour and Champagne lighting.

---

## 1. Interaction Modes

### Micro Orbit
- slow, damped rotation
- never spins freely

### Guided Transformation
- 3-step reveal:
  1. current
  2. planned
  3. final smile

### Layer Peel
- drag reveals enamel → dentine → veneer → aligner

---

## 2. Lighting Modes

- Studio (precision)
- Golden Hour (cosmetic storytelling)
- Inkfall (cinematic immersion)

---

## 3. Material Mapping

Uses:
- enamel.natural
- lithium disilicate
- zirconia multi-layer

Supports translucency gradients, HDR reflections, and subtle edge bloom.
