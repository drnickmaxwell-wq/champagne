# Champagne Prosthetic Anatomy Engine

A canon for generating veneers and crowns that are anatomically correct AND visually Champagne.

---

## 1. Anatomy Dimensions

### Macro Form
- silhouette
- length/width proportion
- incisal curve

### Meso Form
- lobe transitions
- developmental depressions
- line angle shaping

### Micro Form
- texture
- perikymata hints
- incisal halo behaviour

All are adjustable but must remain subtle and natural.

---

## 2. Veneer Style Families

### Natural Enhanced
- Subtle lengthening
- Light texture
- Value shift +0.5
ID: `anatomy.veneer.naturalEnhanced`

### Subtle Glam
- Smoother texture
- More even incisal edge
- Value +1.0
ID: `anatomy.veneer.subtleGlam`

### Ultra-Refined
- Highly regularised edges
- Minimal texture
- Still avoids fake “piano keys”
ID: `anatomy.veneer.ultraRefined`

---

## 3. Crown Profiles

### Posterior
- functional cusp heights
- softened fossae
ID: `anatomy.crown.posterior.functionalSoft`

### Anterior
- enamel-mimicking translucency
ID: `anatomy.crown.anterior.enamelMimic`
