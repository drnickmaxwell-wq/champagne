# Champagne Narrative Flow Engine

A cinematic storytelling framework governing multi-step dental journeys.

---

## 1. Narrative Chapters

### Invitation  
Warm, dawn lighting, haze.

### Discovery  
Midday clarity, cool precision.

### Transformation  
Golden hour, caustics, cinematic warmth.

### Commitment  
Gold micro-bloom, ceremonial tone.

### Assurance  
Inkfall, calming, high clarity.

---

## 2. Narrative Laws

1. Motion must serve meaning.
2. No sliding transitions (fade only).
3. Each chapter inherits Temporal + Emotional lighting state.
4. Every chapter ends with a micro-settle.
