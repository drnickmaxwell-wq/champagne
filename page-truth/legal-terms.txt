Using this website
Overview
These website terms explain how you may use this site and what the information here is (and isn’t) intended for.

The content on this website is provided for general information only. It is not a substitute for a dental examination, diagnosis, or personalised advice.

If you have a dental problem, pain, swelling, trauma, or anything you’re worried about, please contact the practice so we can advise you appropriately.

=== SECTION: accordion ===
What this page covers
These terms apply to your use of this website. They cover the general informational nature of the content, responsible use of the site, and how to contact us if you have questions.

They do not replace the terms you agree to when you become a patient, and they do not form a clinical relationship on their own.
Key points in plain English
- You can browse and use this website for personal, lawful purposes.
- The information is general and may not apply to your specific situation.
- Dentistry often needs an examination and sometimes X-rays or scans before advice is reliable.
- If you need urgent help, please contact us rather than relying on website information.
Your choices and controls
You are welcome to print or share pages for personal use, but please don’t copy large parts of the site for commercial use.

If you’re making decisions about treatment, the safest route is always an appointment where we can assess you properly and explain options, limits, and likely maintenance.
How to contact us
If you have questions about the website content, or you believe something is unclear or out of date, please contact the practice and we’ll do our best to help.

For clinical concerns (pain, swelling, broken teeth, lost restorations, or anything urgent), please call us so we can guide you to the right next step.
Updates to this page
We may update these website terms from time to time to reflect changes to the site or how we provide information.

The current version will always be available on this page.
