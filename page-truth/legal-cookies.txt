Cookies and website tools
Overview
This page explains how cookies and similar technologies may be used on our website. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help a site work properly, remember preferences, and (where enabled) provide usage insights. Some cookies are essential for core site functions and security. Others may be used for analytics to understand which pages are visited and how the site performs. Optional cookies, if used, can be managed through your browser settings. You can also manage cookies through your browser settings, although this may affect how some features behave.
