=== SECTION: treatment.faq ===
Implant restoration FAQs
Planning, fit, and materials
Can I see the design before it is made?
Yes. We share digital previews and, where useful, printed mock-ups so you can understand contours and contacts.
Do you often print these restorations?
We select printing, milling, or lab ceramics based on strength, aesthetics, and your long-term needs. Porcelain or hybrid ceramics may be recommended for higher-load cases.
How do you check the bite?
We map contacts digitally and verify them at the fit appointment, making measured refinements to protect the implant and surrounding teeth.
What follow-up is needed?
Youâ€™ll have scheduled reviews to monitor tissues, contacts, and wear. We also outline home care to keep the restoration clean.
Are the materials regulated?
Yes. We use regulated dental materials selected for their intended indications and explain their maintenance and lifespan.
