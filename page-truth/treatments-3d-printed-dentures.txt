=== SECTION: treatment.faq ===
Denture FAQs
Clarity before you proceed
How fast can 3D printed dentures be made?
Turnaround is typically faster than traditional methods because scanning and in-house printing reduce lab transit. We confirm timelines after your assessment.
Will they feel different to traditional dentures?
Digital design aims for improved comfort and retention. We plan try-ins and adjustments to ensure the fit is right for you.
Can colours be customised?
Yes. We offer gum characterisation and tooth shades guided by your clinician to keep results natural.
What if I need a reline or repair?
Stored digital files let us reprint or adjust efficiently, with the clinician checking bite and pressure points to avoid repeating issues.
